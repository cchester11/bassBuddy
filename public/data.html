Summer Fishing
Fish deep and aggressively. Summer fish can also hang out in shallow coves
Winter Fishing
Fish for schools. Bass will typically school around deep structure in the winter.
Spring Fishing
Fish during the spring are aggressively feeding and protecting their hatchlings. Bed fishing is effective but frowned upon in the fishing community
Fall Fishing
Bass are bulking up to prepare for the winter this time of year. Fish with lighter tackle like plastic worms to throw the most cast and get the most bites
Crankbait or Spinnerbait fishing
Good to use Medium to Medium Light rods. You want the rod to have a lot of bend in it. Use reels with low gear ratios like a 5:4:1
Senko and Jig fishing
Medium Heavy to Heavy rods. Use high gear ratio reels like 6:8:1 to 7:5:1
